About our company

Who we are...

RING ELD is a Texas-based company specializing in providing top-tier ELD (Electronic Logging Device) compliance solutions for truck drivers and fleet operators. We know how challenging it can be to meet the strict FMCSA (Federal Motor Carrier Safety Administration) requirements, and that’s why we’ve built a platform designed to simplify these tasks.

Our platform combines cutting-edge technology with a user-friendly interface, making it easy for anyone to stay compliant. Whether you're a driver or a fleet manager, our goal is to provide you with a reliable, straightforward tool that takes the complexity out of ELD compliance.
ELD PORTAL

How we can help

Our ELD solution is designed to simplify every aspect of compliance. With our easy-to-use app and reliable hardware, you can effortlessly manage driving hours and prevent violations. The platform is highly customizable, allowing you to adapt the electronic logbook to your specific needs, ensuring accuracy and compliance at all times.

In addition to ELD, we offer other key features to support your operations:

  • IFTA mileage tracking – Accurately track fuel usage and distances across states for effortless IFTA (International Fuel Tax Agreement) reporting.
  • VIR (Driver Vehicle Inspection Reports) – Conduct and record pre- and post-trip vehicle inspections to ensure your fleet stays safe and meets regulatory standards.
  • User-friendly logbooks – Our electronic logbooks are easy to adjust and update, giving drivers the flexibility they need while staying compliant.

Advanced fleet management

Our solution goes beyond ELD compliance, offering a comprehensive fleet management system that provides full visibility and control over your fleet. With our 4G/LTE GPS tracking, you can monitor vehicles in real-time, ensuring optimal routing and operational efficiency.

Some key benefits of our fleet management system include:

  • Real-time fleet monitoring – Track your fleet’s locations, driving behavior, and routes in real time to increase productivity and reduce fuel costs
  • Maintenance tracking – Stay ahead of breakdowns and unexpected repairs with automated reminders for routine maintenance, ensuring your vehicles are always in top condition.
  • Intuitive management tools – Our easy-to-navigate portal offers complete fleet oversight, allowing you to manage driver logs, vehicle health, and compliance effortlessly from anywhere.
